Yujin Chen 陈雨劲

I'm a Ph.D. student at the Technical University of Munich, working with Prof. Matthias Nießner in the Visual Computing Lab. Before that, I received my B.Eng and M.Sc from Wuhan University.

My current research interests lie in 3D reconstruction, 3D generation, scene understanding, neural rendering, motion analysis, and representation learning.
