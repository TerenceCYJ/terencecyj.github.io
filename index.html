Yujin Chen

I am a second-year master student working with Prof. Zhigang Tu and Prof. Ruizhi Chen in State Key Laboratory of LIESMARS at Wuhan University, China. BeforeI that, I received my B.Eng degree in Geomatics Engineering from Wuhan University in 2018. My research was on photogrammetry, image geo-localization and indoor positioning.

My research interest lies in computer vision, human sensing and deep learning. My current focus is on hand/human/object pose and shape estimation.

I am fortunate to work with Prof. Junsong Yuan at University at Buffalo, State University of New York.
